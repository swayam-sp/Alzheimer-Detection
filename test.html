  • • Diabetes & hypertension

  • • Physical inactivity
  • • Poor diet & sleep

Prevention Strategies

While there's no guaranteed prevention, research shows these strategies may help reduce risk:

Physical Health

  • • Regular aerobic exercise (150 min/week)
  • • Manage cardiovascular health
  • • Control blood pressure & cholesterol
  • • Maintain healthy weight

Lifestyle Factors

  • • Mediterranean-style diet
  • • Quality sleep (7-9 hours/night)
  • • Limit alcohol & avoid smoking
  • • Mental stimulation & learning
